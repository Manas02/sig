CompChem WG Meet #1.

Greetings !

On Tuesday, October 10th 2023, ACSBCP-SIG held its introductory event that too its first ever Computational Chemistry Working Group Meet!

Darshan Barhate, T.Y. B.Pharm student, presented the paper titled ‘Genes mirroring geography within Europe’ by Novembre et.al. Darshan presented how genetic and geographical variation are interrelated and what are the possible implications of these findings for disease research & ancestral locations of individuals.

Manas Mahale , also a T.Y.Bpharm student, was next to present the paper ‘AutoDock Vina: Improving the Speed and Accuracy of Docking with a New Scoring Function, Efficient Optimization, and Multithreading’ by Trott and Olson. Manas introduced and guided the audience through foundational principles of molecular docking and scoring functions. He expanded upon the use cases for docking as well as the failure in generalisation of scoring function.

ACSBCP-SIG’s inaugural meet was successful in establishing an interactive discussion with the audience and thus concluded with a splendid response. We thank all the attendees and look forward to having you at our next meeting !
