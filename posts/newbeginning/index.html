Greetings !

Welcome to the Special Interest Group at Bombay College of Pharmacy (BCPSIG). We are a dynamic and innovative community of aspiring pharmaceutical researchers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of scientific discovery in the world of pharmaceuticals.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and collaborate in the field of pharmaceutical research. We provide a platform for students and researchers to come together, share knowledge, and engage in cutting-edge research projects.

We aim to build and create:

  • Passionate Community: Join a passionate group of individuals who share your love for pharmaceutical research.
  • Research Opportunities: Gain hands-on experience through our research projects and gain valuable skills.
  • Networking Opportunities: Build connections with like-minded peers and industry professionals.
  • Academic Resources: Access to valuable resources, workshops, and seminars.
