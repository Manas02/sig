It is our great pleasure to report the inaugural session of the Special Interst Group at BCP was successfully conducted. This post will briefly touch upon the highlights of the event.

Introduction to SIG @ BCP

SIG, a novelty at BCP was introduced with all its facets including the Working Groups of SIG which are collaborative and nurturing collective of people interested in same / similar niche research areas. These groups are formed and will continue to form based on the research interst of SIG members. The working groups are formed on the basis of data collected from the SIG Membership Form. The data collected will be used for the sole purpose of understanding the dynamics of research oriented student population and can be made available for anyone to access upon reasonable request.
